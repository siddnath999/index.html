Air-Fried Korean Chicken Wings

These Korean-style air-fried wings are crispy, sticky and spicy. Cooked in an air fryer they are ready in less than 30 minutes.

  • Preheat air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  • Place wings in a large bowl and toss with salt, garlic powder, onion powder and black pepper.
  • Dip each wing in the sauce and garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds.
